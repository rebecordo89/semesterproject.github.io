Thomas Edison

Thomas Alva Edison was born february 11, 1847 and he was known for the invention of the lightbulb. When Thomas younger he lived in Michigan and didnt spend much time in school. Due to this he was homeschooled, Here he taught himself how to read. Starting at the age of 13 he started working as a Newsboy and spent his free time reading scientific and technical books.
